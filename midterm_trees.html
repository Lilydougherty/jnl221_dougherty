Looking ahead, the Bronx is expected to see over 1000 tree plantings in the December 2025 season--a 60% increase from the previous planting season. No tree plantings are planned for Manhattan or Staten Island in the January 2026 season, while Queens is scheduled for over 2,700. In the June 2026 season, six are scheduled for Manhattan--3 on West End Ave., 2 on West 61 St., and 1 on West 71 St.--all in cit council district six.
